Output tokens per task

GLM-5.2 emits the most output tokens of any leading open-weights model. Lower per-token cost, but more tokens to wait for.

{% include components/prose-center.html text='At max thinking effort, GLM-5.2 emits 43k output tokens per Intelligence Index task (37k reasoning, 6k answer). For comparison, MiniMax-M3 emits 24k, Kimi K2.6 emits 35k, and DeepSeek V4 Pro (max) emits 37k. GLM-5.2 is roughly 65% to 80% more verbose than the leanest open-weights peers.' %} {% include components/prose-center.html text='For batch jobs and overnight pipelines, this is irrelevant. That is a real trade-off, not a footnote.' %}
