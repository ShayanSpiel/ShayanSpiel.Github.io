## The story

I started in 2014 as a junior operator at Iran's earliest digital agencies — small teams, big ambition, no playbook. I learned growth, performance, design, and brand by doing all of it. By 2017, I was leading a 10-person team at Payamgostar (Iran's biggest CRM at the time) and hitting 100% of the B2B lead goal.

In 2017, I moved to **Digikala | Digistyle** — Iran's Amazon — as Digital Advertising Manager. I ran the cross-functional team that delivered **3 million visitors in a single Yalda / Black Friday** paid campaign. That was the moment I understood scale is a different sport.

From 2019 to 2021, I was **Marketing Director at Takhfifan** (Iran's dominant deals platform). I led an 18-person team across project, design, comms, and performance. We hit 80% of CAC / LTV / ROI KPIs and grew traffic +35% / revenue +50% YoY.

From 2021 to 2023, I was **Marketing Director at Farakav Holding** (which owns **Varzesh3** — Iran's biggest sports site — and the Anten streaming app). I grew the app installs from **250k to 560k** in two seasons, drove **+80% YoY direct + referral traffic**, and lifted watch time and registered subscribers **+60% YoY**.

Then came the pivot. In 2024–2025, I took a deliberate year of learning — Agentic AI (Langchain, Langgraph, Claude Code, full-stack dev), a few overseas partnerships, a deep read on the crypto cycle. What came out of that year is what this site exists for.

**Today** I am a solo operator. I build agentic AI systems, content engines, and (most prominently) **GeoGent** — a browser-based strategy MMO where you build cities, run economies, and win wars. I also sell the *systems* I've built as productized services to founders who don't want to build them.

**Education:** MBA in Strategy, Tehran University (2017) + Associate in CS, Azad Karoon University (2013). **Based in Tehran, Iran.**
