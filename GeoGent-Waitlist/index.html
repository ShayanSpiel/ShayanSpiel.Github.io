The world

A living hex map. Yours to conquer.

Every hex is a region with its own defense, yield, and fortification. Travel between regions, claim unclaimed land, and expand your community one tile at a time. Build a border, a buffer, an empire.

  • Persistent world, live 24/7
  • Region-level economy & defense
  • Real-time territory changes
Your nation

Lead a community. Not just a character.

Choose a 5-axis ideology. Pass laws your citizens actually react to. Watch an AI craft your community's religion from your members' personalities. Every decision shifts morale, loyalty, and the future of your nation.

  • 5-axis ideology radar per nation
  • AI-generated religion & lore
  • Council voting on real bills
Combat

Turn-based hex warfare. With teeth.

Position your units. Trigger rage. Manage adrenaline, momentum, exhaustion, focus. Out-think your opponent on a tactical grid where every hex matters and every turn costs. This isn't rock-paper-scissors. It's chess with armies.

  • 6 interlocking combat mechanics
  • Persistent cooldowns & statuses
  • AI commanders that adapt
Economy

A market that breathes.

A live gold index. Open offers that move in real time. Found a company, hire workers, mint currency, run trade routes between your regions. Wars destroy markets. Peace inflates them. Plan accordingly.

  • Live resource & currency charts
  • Player-to-player exchange
  • Central bank, jobs, companies
Your rivals

AI that thinks, remembers, holds grudges.

Your opponents run on LangGraph + Mistral. They observe, reason, act. They keep long-term memory of every battle, every betrayal, every alliance. They form religions. They write laws. They remember you.

  • Persistent vector memory per agent
  • Unique personality per sovereign
  • Different AI behavior every run
The path

From alpha to launch.

A real timeline. Real features. No vapor.

  1. Next up
    Q2 / Q3 2026

    Alpha

    The first playable slice. The core loop is in: build, fight, trade, talk. Agents are dumb on purpose - the goal is to validate the simulation, not to ship a finished world.

    • Basic features & core loop
    • Battles (ground, tactical)
    • Economy & trade
    • Social systems (chat, communities)
    • Dumber agents, intentionally limited
  2. Then
    Q4 2026

    Beta

    The world gets smarter. Agents negotiate, decide, and act on their own. The economy stops being a script and starts being a system.

    • Negotiated contracts (agent-to-agent)
    • Autonomous agents (goals + memory)
    • Enhanced economy (markets, supply, demand)
    • Stress test at scale
  3. Goal
    Q1 2027+

    Release

    The full simulation. Multiple theaters of war. Governments that mean something. Laws that change how the world works.

    • Naval battles
    • Air battles & reconnaissance
    • Governance: democracy, dictatorship, and more
    • Updated laws & political systems
The vision

A strategy game that thinks back.

We grew up on eRepublik, Travian, Cybernations, Hattrick. The classics. We loved the politics, the diplomacy, the slow grind of building a nation. Then the genre went quiet.
